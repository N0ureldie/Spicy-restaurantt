Recipe Of Pasta

Cook your favorite pasta according to package instructions. In a skillet, sauté minced garlic in olive oil, add crushed tomatoes, dried basil, oregano, salt, and pepper; simmer for 10 minutes. Toss cooked pasta in the sauce, garnish with fresh basil.

Recipe of Pasta With Sauce

Cook pasta according to package instructions; drain and set aside. In a pan, sauté garlic in olive oil, add canned crushed tomatoes, red pepper flakes, salt, and cook for 15 minutes. Combine the sauce with pasta, garnish with fresh basil, and serve.

Recipe Of Egg

2 eggs 1 tablespoon butter or cooking oil Salt and pepper to taste 1/4 cup diced bell peppers (any color) 1/4 cup diced tomatoes 2 tablespoons diced onions Optional: grated cheese, chopped herbs (parsley, chives), or a splash of milk for fluffier eggs
